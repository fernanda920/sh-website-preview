Who we are · Sales-only recruiters

We've carried a quota. Now we find the people who can.

Sellers Hub is a sales-only recruitment firm. For over a decade we've placed the sales talent that actually moves revenue, onshore in the U.S. and offshore globally, for B2B companies that live and die by pipeline.

Our story

Operators, not resume-shufflers.

Most recruiters have never carried a number. We have. That is the difference behind every search we run: we recognize the people who can sell because we have been them.

We only do sales. Not a generalist agency with a sales desk, a firm built end to end around go-to-market talent, across manufacturing, commercial insurance, and B2B tech, onshore and offshore.

And we don't wait for applicants. We run every search like an outbound sales motion, mapping the market and reaching the passive talent that never hits a job board, then scoring every candidate against our SELLER framework before you ever meet them.
