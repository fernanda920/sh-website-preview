Questions, answered

Global talent FAQ

How much does offshore sales talent cost?

Offshore GTM hires run roughly 60 to 80% less than a comparable U.S. hire. You can engage on a subscription from $900/mo or as a one-time direct hire. Try the ROI calculator for your numbers.

How fast can you fill an offshore sales role?

Most offshore searches fill in a few weeks, faster than a typical U.S. search, because we headhunt against your exact role rather than waiting for applicants.

What roles can you place offshore?

The full go-to-market org: SDRs and BDRs, Account Executives, Account Managers, Customer Success, RevOps, and GTM Engineers.

How do you vet offshore candidates?

Every candidate is scored against our SELLER framework and video-interviewed against your specific role before you ever see them, the same standard we apply onshore.

Will offshore reps understand the U.S. market?

We screen for real experience selling into the U.S., and our team has sold into that market firsthand, so we know what to look for.

What if a placement doesn't work out?

Offshore placements carry a full replacement guarantee. But we do the work upfront to get the hire right the first time, the guarantee is our backstop, not our strategy.
