Insurance recruitment FAQs

How long does it take to hire a commercial insurance producer?

Most commercial insurance producer roles are filled in under 60 days. Sellers Hub runs retained searches with a dedicated team, weekly calibration calls, and full pipeline visibility to keep hiring timelines predictable and efficient.

Why is hiring commercial insurance producers so difficult?

Hiring commercial insurance producers is difficult because top performers are typically not active job seekers. Sellers Hub specializes in recruiting passive candidates who are already producing at a high level, helping brokerages access talent that is not available through job postings.

How does Sellers Hub approach commercial insurance staffing differently?

The insurance sales recruitment team at Sellers Hub is fully committed to filling the role from day one. This model provides accountability, consistent communication, and a structured search process, unlike contingency recruiting where speed often outweighs quality.

Can Sellers Hub support multi-location or national insurance hiring?

Yes. Sellers Hub supports multi-location and national commercial insurance staffing needs. Our sales recruitment services are especially valuable for brokerages expanding into new territories or integrating acquisitions that require multiple producer hires at once.

What types of insurance roles does Sellers Hub recruit for?

Sellers Hub recruits for revenue-generating roles including commercial insurance producers, account executives, account managers, sales leaders, and new business producers. The focus is always on roles tied directly to pipeline and revenue growth.

How does Sellers Hub ensure quality when hiring insurance producers?

Sellers Hub uses the proprietary SELLER framework to evaluate candidates across sales performance, industry expertise, learning ability, execution, and role fit. This ensures candidates are aligned with the brokerage's market, sales cycle, and growth strategy.
