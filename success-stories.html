Logistics · Supply Chain Tech

The rare Solutions Architect that changed everything, in 12 weeks.

BlueCargo.ai was scaling fast, but late-stage deals had turned highly technical and engineers kept getting pulled into sales calls. With no dedicated Solutions Architect, product development slowed and sales cycles stretched. The role required both logistics domain knowledge and enterprise SaaS technical sales, based in New York or LA.

We went vertical. We targeted freight-audit platforms, TMS vendors, and supply-chain SaaS providers, then only advanced candidates who had actually led customer architecture reviews and navigated complex multi-stakeholder implementations.

Commercial Insurance · Top-20 Brokerage

A consistent producer hiring engine, across five regions.

A top-20 national brokerage was balancing aggressive acquisition growth with the need to build sustainable, organic revenue through producer hiring, across five regions at once. Passive talent, compensation risk, false positives, and multi-region complexity all at the same time.

We went vertical. Sellers Hub ran it as nationwide talent access plus behavioral benchmarking, with region-specific targeting and regional comp benchmarking, screening producers on validated production versus inherited accounts.
