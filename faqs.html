Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions.

Straight answers on how we work, what it costs, the guarantee, and global talent.

About Sellers Hub

What does Sellers Hub do?

Sellers Hub is a sales-only recruitment firm. We place high-performing sales talent onshore in the U.S. and offshore globally, for B2B companies that need pipeline, closed deals, and revenue.

What makes Sellers Hub different?

We only do sales, and we run candidate outreach the way you would run an outbound motion, headhunting passive performers rather than waiting for applicants. Nine out of ten of our clients come back for a second search.

Which industries do you specialize in?

Commercial insurance, manufacturing, and B2B tech and SaaS, across the full go-to-market org, onshore and offshore.

Our process & vetting

How do you vet candidates?

Every candidate is scored against our SELLER framework through a structured, two-step interview against the criteria for your specific role, so you meet a rounded professional, not a number on a resume.

How fast can you fill a role?

Most searches reach a signed hire in about four to six weeks, because we headhunt the market directly instead of waiting on applicants.

Do you headhunt passive candidates?

Yes. The best sellers are not applying. We map the market and reach them directly with a real, multi-channel outreach motion.

Pricing & guarantee

What does it cost?

U.S. searches are retained, on a percentage-based fee we scope with you on the discovery call. Global and offshore talent engages on a subscription from $900/mo or as a one-time direct hire.

What is the guarantee?

Placements carry a full replacement guarantee, commonly 120 days. It is backed by getting the hire right upfront, a backstop, not a substitute for doing the work.

Global talent & offshore

How much does offshore sales talent cost?

Roughly 60 to 80% less than a comparable U.S. hire, on a subscription from $900/mo or a one-time direct hire.

What roles can you place offshore?

The full GTM org: SDRs and BDRs, Account Executives, Account Managers, Customer Success, RevOps, and GTM Engineers.

Will offshore reps understand the U.S. market?

We screen for real experience selling into the U.S., and we vet offshore candidates on the same SELLER standard we apply onshore.
